You were in this world long before I was.

You came first. And then you disappeared — not by choice, not by fault. Just… gone. And all that remained was an invisible emptiness — the one I was born into, never knowing what it was missing.

When I was 15, someone said your name to me for the first time. Casually. As if it was nothing. As if it didn’t matter.

I remember thinking: “This can’t be real. It must be a mistake. A joke.” But something inside me trembled. My heart ached in a way that made me realize: it already knew you existed.

And then — silence. No photos. No letters. No trace. Just this strange, unexplainable feeling: that somewhere out there, someone lived, someone I was tied to by blood and spirit and story, someone I’d never met, but never forgot.

You are my brother. You didn’t become my brother — you always were. I just couldn’t reach you until now.

And now, you're here. Real. Alive. As if time folded inward, and we were simply turned away from each other — until now, when we finally turned back and saw.

You are someone I’ve carried within me all my life, without knowing your face. And now I see it — warm, joyful, strong. Real.

I don’t know what comes next. But I do know this: this is not the end. This is a beginning. A return. A chapter where you no longer vanish.

I made this site so that you would know: Even in silence — you were loved. Even in absence — you were never forgotten. Even before we met — I was waiting for you.

Welcome home, brother.

With love,

your sister
